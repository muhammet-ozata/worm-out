Worm Out! - Privacy Policy

Kids Edition — Puzzle Game

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Worm Out! (the "App"), developed and published by Muhammet Özata ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

Worm Out! is a free-to-play puzzle game designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Key Point: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. All game data is stored locally on your device only.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent. Worm Out! does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email address, phone number, physical address, etc.)
  • Require user registration, accounts, or login of any kind
  • Collect or access device identifiers for our own purposes
  • Use analytics or tracking tools to monitor user behavior
  • Collect location data (GPS, IP-based geolocation, or otherwise)
  • Access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or files
  • Collect or process any biometric data
  • Transmit any data to our servers (we do not operate any servers)
  • Share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties

3. Locally Stored Data

The App stores a small amount of non-personal game data exclusively on your device using the device's local storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Game progress: Current level, maximum level reached per category, and star ratings earned
  • User preferences: Selected avatar icon, chosen puzzle category, sound/music toggle settings, text-to-speech voice preference, vibration preference, and locale/language selection
  • Game mode: Zen mode (relaxed play) toggle state

This data is purely functional and required for the App to remember your game progress and settings. It contains no personally identifiable information. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

4. No Payments or In-App Purchases

Worm Out! is a completely free application. We do not offer any in-app purchases, subscriptions, premium features, virtual currencies, or any other paid content. No financial information (credit card numbers, bank details, payment tokens) is ever collected or processed by the App.

5. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC. These ads help us keep the App free for all users.

5.1 Types of Ads

  • Interstitial ads: Full-screen ads that may appear between levels (not on every level, and never during the first few levels of gameplay)
  • Rewarded ads: Optional video ads that the user can choose to watch in exchange for in-game benefits (such as extra lives). These are never forced.

5.2 AdMob and Data

Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve and personalize ads, including device identifiers, IP address, and general usage data. This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy, not ours. We do not have access to or control over data collected by AdMob.

For more information about how Google handles data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob & Privacy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

5.3 Ad Personalization & Opt-Out

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off "Personalized Ads"

6. Text-to-Speech Feature

The App uses your device's built-in text-to-speech (TTS) engine to read aloud letters, numbers, and shapes during gameplay. This feature operates entirely on your device using the operating system's native TTS capabilities. No audio data, voice recordings, or speech data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Worm Out! is designed for children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

Our commitment to children's privacy:
  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction)
  • The App does not require any personal information to function
  • There are no social features, chat functionality, user-generated content, or any way for users to communicate with each other through the App
  • There are no external links within the gameplay that could redirect children to other websites or apps (except for ad content served by Google AdMob)
  • The App does not use behavioral tracking or create user profiles

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has somehow provided personal information through the App, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to address your concern.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — For displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts (Fredoka) — The App bundles the Fredoka font. No network requests are made to Google Fonts servers at runtime; the font is embedded in the App.

Apart from Google AdMob's data practices (described in Section 5), no third-party service used by the App collects user data.

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is minimal data security risk associated with the App. The locally stored game progress and preferences are protected by the standard security measures of your device's operating system. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date to benefit from the latest security patches.

10. Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored by the App resides solely on your device. We do not retain any data on external servers. You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data/cache through your device's application settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

Upon deletion, all locally stored game progress and preferences are permanently removed.
